# PRIVACY POLICY REQUIREMENTS

Include clear sections:

1. Information We Collect
   - Contact form details (name, email, company, message)
   - Job application information (resume, contact details, employment history)
   
2. How We Use Information
   - Responding to inquiries
   - Evaluating candidates
   - Client communication purposes

3. Data Handling & Protection
   - Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards
   - Confidential handling of applicant and client information

4. Third-Party Form Providers
   - Clarify that embedded form services may process submitted data

5. Data Retention
   - Retained only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes

6. Data Subject Rights
   - Right to access
   - Right to correction
   - Right to request deletion
   - Contact email for privacy concerns

7. Compliance Statement
   - State that the company adheres to the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173)

Not overly complex.
